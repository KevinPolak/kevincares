Questions

The honest
answers.

How long does Kevin take to give feedback?

Kevin aims to respond within 5–7 days of receiving your submitted essay. You'll always receive a personal reply — this is the entire value proposition.

Do I need prior psychology knowledge?

None. The course is your introduction to these frameworks through lived experience, not academic study. If you've done therapy before, it deepens what you already know.

Can I do this alongside ongoing therapy?

Yes, and it often enhances the therapeutic process. This is not a replacement for professional mental health support — it is a complementary self-knowledge practice.

What language should I submit in?

Any language Kevin reads: English, Chinese, Dutch, French, or Spanish. Submit in whichever allows you to be most honest. Depth of reflection matters more than formality.

Is my work confidential?

Only Kevin reads your submissions. Your work is private. There is no group, no cohort, no shared space. What you write stays between you and Kevin.

What if I get stuck?

You can contact Kevin with course-related questions between submissions. Genuine confusion always gets a response. The course is designed to be challenging, not impossible.
